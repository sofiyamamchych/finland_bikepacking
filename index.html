This blog is dedicated to bicycle trip through Southern Finland that me (Sofiya) and my husband Pavlo have undertaken in summer of 2016. It was our first long-term bicycle journey, and it was really good. So we want to share our experience and tips with readers and maybe even inspire someone to do a similar tour.

Why Finland?

  • Advanced bicycle infrastructure. Vast network of cycling routes throughout the country.
  • Mild weather in summer: it’s never too hot to cycle. Actually, during most days you’ll have to pedal harder not to get too cold!
  • Extremely long summer days. Sun sets at around 23:30 in August.
  • Beautiful northern nature. Finland is the most forested country in Europe: 70% of its territory is covered with forests. 188 000 lakes, the most water in relation to landmass in the world.
  • Freedom to roam the land. You can camp everywhere, even on private property.
  • The cleanest air in Europe.
  • One of the safest countries in the world.
  • Saunas are everywhere: in apartments, summer houses, and even campings. What can be better than to relax in sauna after a long day in the saddle (and finally to get warm!).
Nature. Sport. Infrastructure

But mostly we were inspired by brilliant videos from visitfinland.com They made us dreaming about staying for the night in a tent near Baltic sea shore. Enjoy it too!
